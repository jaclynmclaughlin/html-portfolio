Jaqui's Favorite Movies of All Time

The movies I rewatch over and over again...

Practical Magic

Simply the best fictional movie that depicts modern witchcraft, with magik, a fun romantic mystery plot, and fantastic representations of female relationships.

The School for Good and Evil

A truly fantastic modern fairy tale that turns upside down the expectations and cliches, while also showing us that romantic/sexual love isn't always the most inmporatant kind of love in someone's life.

The Jurassic Park Franchise

My go-to for exciting action adventure movies every time. Made a thousand times better and more enjoyable because dinosaurs.

Enjoy!
